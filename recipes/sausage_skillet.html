Easy Smoked Sausage Skillet

You'll have dinner on the table in no time with this one-skillet sausage and veggie dish served over rice.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 14 ounce package of Hillshire Farms smoked sausage, diagonally cut into 1/4 inch slices
  • 1 large red bell pepper
  • 1 small yellow onion
  • 1 10 ounce package frozen broccoli, thawed
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth or water
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups instant rice
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil and crushed garlic in a large skillet. Stir in smoked sausage slices and cook until browned on all sides.
  2. Add pepper, onion, broccoli, chicken broth, and tomato sauce. Simmer until vegetables are tender and the liquid is absorbed, about 10 minutes.
  3. Cook rice according to package directions while vegetables are cooking.
  4. Stir cooked rice into the skillet, sprinkle with cheese, and serve warm.
